#Include - A techno-learning club associated with talent, knowledge and learning is the official club of Information Technology Department.

We are a collaboration of ardent technophiles and zealous learners who believe that the central purpose of education, learning and collaboration is to mediate voluntary evolution. We believe that an investment in knowledge is the best thing an individual can do. We try to provide the best possible environment for students to reach their maximum potential and to chisel their abilities to the best of their capacities.
